Victory Sher

by Michael Macfadden

Please introduce yourself to our readers. What would you like them to know about you?

I am Vicky – creator of World’s First Meditation NFTs. Currently living in Munich, Germany. 

What was the inspiration for this piece?

All my artwork is based on ThetaArt Technology by Julia Schulz.
THETAART is a method which allows to create beautiful images with high vibrations, that makes them static or animated meditations. 

People who meditate on a regular basis or make other spiritual practice, usually feel how special these images are. 

Can you tell us about your workflow (how do you come up with ideas, what tools do you use to create)?

It takes from 1 up to 3 hours to create one picture. It has many layers and hidden meanings. First I do a meditation to bring my mind in Theta condition. After that I open a program and start to create.

I am using the Autodesk Sketch Book program. To make animated images and GIFs I am using some other picture and video redactors.
