NippyNip2

by Michael Macfadden

Please introduce yourself to our readers:

Hey I’m Nippy born and raised in Australia Melbourne, grew up skating and listening to music, Allot of my art has been inspired by what I’ve seen/felt/heard/thought over these years. 

How long have you been creating in the digital realm and what tools do you use to do so?

I’ve been making digital art for about 2 years now and nft’s for almost a year, I’m currently working off a cheaper android phone on the app flipaclip with some exceptions, most of my ideas get drafted from a paper sketch and get re worked on flipaclip. 

Who are your artistic influences? 

I’ve had an abundance of inspiration from heaps of artist of all types. To many to want to go into detail.

What got you interested in NFTs/Crypto Art/Digital Collectibles?

My friend Josh Backman brought me into the whole space and showed me some things and helped me get started with minting. From there I just branched out and found what intrigued me. 

Where can we find you online?

You can find my work on Wax.io 
Or also here on Opensea

Anything else you’d like to share?

Thanks heaps for reading and I hope you enjoy the collab!
