SKATECOINWAX

by Michael Macfadden

Hello everyone we are sk8coin, a skate to earn NFT collection on wax. We are bringing skateboarders from all over the world into the metaverse and on to the wax blockchain.

Our mission is to offer a decentralized platform for skateboarders to express themselves in a fun and unique way through blockchain technology. We use in real life skateboarding photos and videos combined with digital cartooning methods to create our NFTs. We are influenced by freethinkers and artistic expression from skateboarders all over the world. We first got into NFTs in 2018 and have been creating on WAX since 2020.

This Art piece was inspired by the freedom of self expression by Greg Valencia doing an ollie kickflip in Laguna beach California!
