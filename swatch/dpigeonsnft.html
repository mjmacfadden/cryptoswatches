DPIGEONSNFT

by Michael Macfadden

Please introduce yourself to our readers:

My name is Fresh, I am the Designer and 1/2 owner at Disguised Pigeons with my best friend Reffy. We are both located in remote Australia and are childhood friends.

What was the inspiration for this piece?

Our inspiration for our submission, was to capture the vibrant colours you traditionally find in swatch/Pantone booklet.

How long have you been creating in the digital realm?

We started our trading card game in October last year, it has been one of the best journeys we have ever had.

Can you tell us about your workflow (what equipment and software do you use to create)?

I generally use a computer and use different software, ranging from AI and Flexisign.

Who are your artistic influences?

My background comes from branding and sign making, I take a lot of what I do everyday and put into our project.

What got you interested in NFTs/Crypto Art/Digital Collectibles?

We started our crypto journey together in March 2021, when we heard about NFT’s we wanted to combine both aspects. We also collect Funko pops in real life, so we wanted to base our project around trading and collecting.

Where can we find you online?

You can follow the project page on Twitter @ DPIGEONSNFT.

Anything else you’d like to share?

We want to personally thank the CryptoSwatch team for collaborating with us and also showing the world how amazing the WAX blockchain is.
