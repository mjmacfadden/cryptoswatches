GALERIA_NFT

by Michael Macfadden

Please introduce yourself to our readers:

Hi guys thanks for having me my name is Ordonez I’m a house music dj & producer from Ecuador and i run La Galeria NFT. A gallery that is focused on bringing third world country artists to the NFT world.

What was the inspiration for this piece?

Gotta love girls right?? They are the main inspiration, pretty much art by themselves, I’ve also been getting really interested in this flashy animations, i guess they remained me of the strobes in a club but i’ve fallen in love with those lately.

How long have you been creating in the digital realm?

I’ve been making house music for 20 years actually, that was the reason why i joined NFTS, but i started my AI collection a couple of months ago. I’ve been working with different kinds of software for my events, creating an editing flyers, making animations, so i gave it a try and start working on my own collection and people seemed to like my taste.

Can you tell us about your workflow?

Well I use a bunch of different stuff depending on the artwork itself, Most of the girls i use are AI generated so i use some AI sites, then i use photoshop add some more layers and textures, and for the animation i usually go to after effects although i used photoshop for this one.

Who are your artistic influences?

I don’t have one to be honest there is so many amazing works around so i guess I’m being influenced by hundreds of big and small artists in the world.
What got you interested in NFTs/Crypto Art/Digital Collectibles?
As I said I got into it to sell my music, when the pandemic hit i lost all of my gigs, events and spent all of my life’s savings paying the bills, in Ecuador theres no financial aid for anybody much less for artists so I needed to find a way to generate income to provide for my family. Later on I discovered the perks of releasing music without a label and all the problems that come with it, and also realized I had a lot of friends that are awesome digital artists and that could do really great with NFTs.
