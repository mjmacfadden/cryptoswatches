Secret Slainte

by Michael Macfadden

Everyone’s Irish on St. Patrick’s day! As such, we’re getting into the spirit by conducting a special drop. If you don’t speak traditional Irish or Scottish Gaelic, the fine folks at Wikipedia have your back: Sláinte means health. It is commonly used as a drinking toast in Ireland, Scotland and the Isle of Man. Today, the word “Sláinte” is featured on our newest Crypto Swatch in a sneaky animation.

So here’s to all of you NFT Collecting, Irish (at least for today) folks out there. Happy St. Patrick’s Day from us to you!
