The 1st of April

by Michael Macfadden

NFTs are super cool. There’s no denying that, but it can be easy to start taking things too seriously. As such, we conducted a poll a few days ago to see how the greater community would feel about an April Fool’s Day NFT. Well, the people voted and the results were overwhelmingly in favor.

So, we’ve created the first prank NFT that we are releasing for free on April 1st—also known as April Fools’ Day. Our hope is that this becomes an annual reminder to keep things light and fun.

Since you’re reading this, you are cool and on the inside. The QR Code directs you to a hidden page on our site that each year will feature a new gag. This year, you get Rick Rolled when you visit.

You’ve been warned.

One last thing: according to TipHero (whatever that is), cayenne is “the color of April.”
