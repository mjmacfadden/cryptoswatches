DAVBORJA0918

by Michael Macfadden

Please introduce yourself to our readers:

My name is Dav Borja from the Philippines, and i do pen arts, i started drawing during my early years, i was a sailor for almost 20 years, then started my own e-commerce business and was shut down because of covid, then i started to make drawings again.

What was the inspiration for this piece?

Guardian dog is an illustration of our pet dog who accompanied us in our everyday life, a friend and part of our family.

How long have you been creating in the digital realm?

About a year and half, I started on NFT because of playing Alien worlds and my first collectible NFT was from Tidbyts duckmoos.

Can you tell us about your workflow (what equipment and software do you use to create)?

Old school pen, pencils and papers, i usually do my coloring manually but now i use ACD software to render colors, edit online tools for videos and gifs and some mobile apps for the effects.

Who are your artistic influences?

I really don’t know, when i was 5, my first drawing was a house and been considered art by the people who knew me

What got you interested in NFTs/Crypto Art/Digital Collectibles?

I’ve been on crypto since 2009, erc-20 era, and because i needed some extra funds for my employees who lost their jobs, I made NFTs for a living and to help fund a foundation to help families.
