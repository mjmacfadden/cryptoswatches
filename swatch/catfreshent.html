Hello readers, I am Catfresh. I want to thank you and say it’s a huge honor to work with you once again. This time we were much more proper.

I am a recovering drug addict and am 7 months and 1 day clean from my drug of choice as of right now 6/8/22.

Professionally I am the sole owner of Catfresh Entertainment LLC, based in Virginia, USA. I am blessed to have multiple streams of income, but it all started in October 2013 when I decided I wanted to work for myself to spend time with my daughter and family. She will be 12 on August 18th, her name is Hope.
