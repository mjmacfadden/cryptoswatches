Please re-introduce yourself to our readers:

Hi! I’m Luca (alias PK) from Italy. I founded CyberDucks project back in March with my girlfriend.
Later, after receiving great feedback from the community, things got serious and we had to add a programmer and a designer to our team. And so we are now: me, Smartis, Aglow and Dora Maar!

Please tell us about the evolution of your project:

I’ve always loved pixel drawings, so our genesis collection couldn’t be otherwise.
After having published the entire Genesis collection, however, we felt the need to try to do something new and totally different.
So the new CyberDucks Evolution were born!

But hey, maybe pixels may come back in the future..
