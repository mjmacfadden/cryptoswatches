BunnyonWorld

by Michael Macfadden

Please introduce yourself to our readers. What would you like them to know about you?

Hello! I am Albert, the creator of Bunnyon World! Right now I live in Spain dedicating all the time I can to my collection of NFTs, from its creation to advertising on social networks.

What was the inspiration for this piece?

Since I have been experimenting with a new color palette for my Bunnyons and the collaboration is a kind of experiment, I have been inspired by doing something similar to a laboratory

How long have you been creating in the digital realm?

In April 2021 I made my first Bunnyon, at that time I was working in a book warehouse and could not dedicate time to the NFTs, but I got tired of that work and since September / October I have been selling my collection. But I have been drawing for myself since I was little

Can you tell us about your workflow (what equipment and software do you use to create)?

I like the simple and I prefer to draw with the phone so I looked for a simple application and I found the Dotpict app, focused on pixel art, and nothing else, drawing pixel by pixel and frame by frame.

Who are your artistic influences?

Pixel art in general, but especially retro games from the 80s and 90s, I really don’t have any specific artist as a reference.

What got you interested in NFTs/Crypto Art/Digital Collectibles?

The possibility of creating and selling art in a totally independent way as well as being able to make crypto games to use the NFTs.

Where can we find you online?

  • On Twitter: twitter.com/BunnyonWorld
  • Main market: neftyBlocks.com/c/bunnyonworld
  • And in AtomicHub and NFTHive wax secondary markets.

Also I have a few exclusive Bunnyon for opensea, hand drawn & randomly mixed: opensea.io/collection/bunnyonworld

Anything else you’d like to share?

I am working to create my own Cryptogame with its own Cryptocurrency, the existing Bunnyon will have passive rewards in the future. I hope to have it prepared in the not too distant future
