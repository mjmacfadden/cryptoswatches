NFTALLEYCAT

by Michael Macfadden

Please introduce yourself to our readers:

Hey how’s it going? I am NFTAlleyCat, or “Catarax”, and I am the Creator of the CryptoCrypt & TeneInfernum Collections. I’m from Los Angeles and have been creating Artwork for 8 years now.

The CryptoCrypt houses NFTs with Individual Lore, Tying into the overlying Realm of the Collection: Netheros. As well, I have started a 1 of 1 Series of Cat Familiars called “Nekos”.

What was the inspiration for this piece?

I was inspired heavily by imagery within Dante’s Inferno, and the Video Game Elden Ring. Tie that in with the Lore I create currently in the Crypt, and it creates a beautifully macabre scene.

How long have you been creating in the digital realm?

My digital art journey has only recently started, with a good portion of my beginning pieces in the CryptoCrypt being Physical Artworks, Scanned and Edited to create the finished product. I had dabbled a little bit in Digital Mediums beforehand, but had not felt I was ready at that point to make the leap.

Can you tell us about your workflow?

I use an iPad Pro, Apple Pencil Gen 1, with Procreate. As for the Brush Packs I use Grit Texture Supply now for mostly everything, and have modified them a bit to my liking.

Who are your artistic influences?

Artistic Influences I would have to say are Junji Ito & Jamie Hewlett

What got you interested in NFTs/Crypto Art/Digital Collectibles?

I found NFTs last March, after a Live Demo by @LushSux on Instagram/Twitter.
I got hooked instantly after a heavy night of research and a rabbit hole of articles.

Where can we find you online?

You can find all of my work on AtomicHub & Neftyblocks. CryptoCrypt2 (Whitelisted) + TeneInfernum (In the Process of WL)

  • CryptoCrypt Neftyblocks: https://neftyblocks.com/c/cryptocrypt2
  • TeneInfernum Atomic Hub: https://wax.atomichub.io/explorer/collection/teneinfernum

Anything else you’d like to share?

Checkout my Collection of 1/1 Nekos on Neftyblocks and the AH Secondary Market. Neko Breeding is coming out soon, once we hit 100 total Nekos. From there, a large portion of Gen 2 Nekos will be created by the Holders of our Gen 1 Nekos.
