TROYS ARTWORK

by Michael Macfadden

Please introduce yourself to our readers :

hey everyone! my name is Troy and i’m from central Canada.

What was the inspiration for this piece?

my project is about creation and connection! its meaningful for me to connect with other creative minds through artwork. i have a deep love for creating artwork, focused mostly on cats and the natural world.

this piece titled Onyx Eve was inspired by the colours and atmosphere of spooky season! it was sketched out with pencil first, and then outlined in ink. i then painted in water colour, and added the finishing touches with pencil crayons.

How long have you been creating in the digital realm?

i have been creating in the digital world for almost a year now.

Can you tell us about your workflow?

all of my artwork is traditional, and i tend to use ink and pencil crayon to create my artworks. i then take photos of each piece with my phone, and that is also where i do all of my editing. once the editing is finalized i upload to my PC where i drop my pieces!

Who are your artistic influences? 

my father Byron the Artist has been a huge artistic influence in my life, as well as all of the lovely creators i follow and see on twitter! my father also inspired my interest in creating NFTs.

Where can we find you online?

you can find my twitter @troysartwork

and you can find my atomichub and neftyblocks @troysartwork !!

Anything else you’d like to share?

a big thank you to CryptoSwatch! i have really appreciated this collab. its been much fun creating this piece, cheers!
