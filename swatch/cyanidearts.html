CyanideArts

by Michael Macfadden

Please introduce yourself to our readers:

Hi my name is Timothy also known as MunK. Born on a cold Friday the 13th in January. Also as a Mental Health Worker, I’ve seen nightmares and good times. I hail from Endicott, New York. Im really into tattoos and I have my ears gauged to 1”. Music is something that takes me away from classic rock, punk, rap, lets just say I like it all. I’m a big horror movie fan with comedy movies mixed in.

What was the inspiration for this piece?

The inspiration to my piece here. Is the dead feeling I have felt inside for away. Each and everyday decay can become a heartache or even things that happen in real life.

How long have you been creating in the digital realm?

I’ve been creating art my whole life mostly. But with NFTs, I started in February of 2022. One that got me somewhat into in was Mobius Strip! For my artwork to happen – I use a iPad with a pencil. For programs it depends I usually use Sketchbook for my PFP projects. With the gif side I use motion leap and filto. When I work on a collage its either Live collage or building something in sketchbook.

Who are your artistic influences?

My artist influences would be some old school like Pink Floyd art or even the horror classics. Music helps like metal and rap.

Where can we find you online?

You can find me on Neftyblocks.com, Atomichub.io, Twitter. I do have stuff on opensea but rarely touch it. I love WaXp. Wax has a great community.
