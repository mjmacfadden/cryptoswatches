In Homage

by Michael Macfadden

Coming of age the 90s, in the greater Chicagoland area, the number 23 had (and still has for that matter) a meaning far geater than just about any other number. As such, we felt it important to do something special with our 23rd Crypto Swatch.

Titled “In Homage – No. 000023,” this single minted swatch will go up for auction on March 23rd at 9:00 PM CST. The auction will last 23 days, and all proceeds will go to programs that support youth athletics in Chicago.

We don’t know what the results of this auction will be, but we are hopeful that it will be positive.
