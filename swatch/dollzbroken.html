DollzBroken

by Michael Macfadden

Please introduce yourself to our readers:

Hello everyone, my name is Ohad and im a digital designer from tel aviv. Ive been doing graphic design for the past 22 years (im 40). Im a geek, a punk, and a old school rpg gamer. I have a huge D&D collection and i also play a lot of old retro games,I guess u can say im a 90s kid that never stopped playing

What was the inspiration for this piece?

A big part of my childhood was with my best friend – the nintendo gameboy. when i relized i can revive that old retro nostalgia an bring it to web 3.0 i knew thats what im ment to do so i started with the waxtrigesHow long have you been creating in the digital realm?

About a year and two months.

Some of my collection is based on actual dollz i twist and turn ( i do lots of crafts) like that evil kid from toystory, so i started with making physical doolz and taking pictures of them, and then drew them digitally.

I’m an adobe ferak, and i work mostly with photoshop, but in the recent weeks ive started toying with aseprite a bit for pixel art, i also sometimes just use a pan and a phone camera
